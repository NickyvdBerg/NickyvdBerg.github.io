Biography

I am a PhD student in applied mathematics at Eindhoven University of Technology under the supervision of dr.ir. R. Duits in the Geometric Learning and Differential Geometry group (GLDG) of the Centre for Analysis, Scientific Computing and Applications (CASA) cluster. My research interests include differential geometry, PDEs, numerical methods, geometric tracking and medical image analysis.
